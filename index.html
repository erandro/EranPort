About

Hi! I'm Eran (pronounced like "Aaron"), a designer & developer from Chicago.

Curious by nature, I have always been fascinated by the immense impact that design and technological advancements have on our lives. Throughout my career, I have become very passionate about the intersection of tech and design and I am always seeking the next challenge in those areas.

Born in Tel-Aviv and raised in a Kibbutz, after graduating from high school, I served as a commander in the military for three years and honed my leadership skills. Following an honorable discharge, I proceeded to work as an independent graphic designer, while earning my bachelor’s degree in landscape architecture, which led to an early career as an urban designer and project manager. It was there that I was first exposed to database building and web design. Upon moving to Chicago, I decided to master the art & science that is full stack web development at Northwestern University where I also taught code as a TA. I spent a couple of years developing desktop applications and leading brand and haptic design, at a Chicago based startup. Currently I'm a Senior Frontend Developer at Dialexa, an IBM Company providing IT services.

Some of my additional passions include running, playing basketball, watching anime and making art.
